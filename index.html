Our Story

Welcome to Custom Crust Kitchen. We believe in the power of incredible food to bring people together, using only the finest ingredients to craft a truly unforgettable pizza experience. Our name is Custom Crust Kitchen because we believe your pizza should be a canvas for your exact cravings. Whether you choose from our specialty menu or build your own masterpiece, it all starts on our signature Neapolitan-style thin crust.

With our combined decades of hospitality and culinary experience, we are equipped to cater to your every need—whether it’s an intimate backyard party, a beautiful wedding reception, a large-scale corporate event, or a vibrant food festival. With an unwavering commitment to fresh, high-end products like premium Grande Mozzarella, we promise to stay true to the authentic art of Italian cuisine, straight from our family to yours.

Angela Germano

Founder & CEO

The entrepreneurial backbone of Custom Crust Kitchen, Angela brings a rich international culinary background and a relentless work ethic to the brand. Originally from Brazil, her journey has spanned continents, exploring the vibrant culinary traditions of both Brazil and Portugal, where she gained invaluable experience working in demanding restaurant environments.

Today, she is a highly respected and well-known business owner in the local community. As Christian's mother-in-law, Angela is the matriarch of this venture, ensuring that Custom Crust Kitchen remains true to its core identity: a genuine family business. Her visionary leadership, deep entrepreneurial experience, and uncompromising standard for quality are what elevate our kitchen from a simple pizzeria to a premium catering experience.

Christian De Brun

Founder & Director of Operations

Christian’s culinary journey began long before he stepped into a professional kitchen. Immigrating to the United States from Uruguay in 2001 at the age of 14, he learned the foundational love of cooking from his mother, who also built her life working in kitchens across the country. Starting from the ground up while still in high school, Christian carried a simple philosophy: whether washing dishes or catering a luxury event, always be the absolute best and make every guest feel at home.

Over the years, he climbed the ranks to General Manager, overseeing operations everywhere from high-volume venues to premium, high-end restaurants. In 2008, his trajectory changed when he immersed himself in authentic Italian cuisine at a legendary, landmark pizzeria in Springfield, Massachusetts. It was there that he truly honed his craft and developed a lifelong obsession with the art of the perfect dough. Today, Custom Crust Kitchen is the culmination of his deep-rooted passion for hospitality and his enduring culinary love.
